BREAK YOUR LIMITS

Limit Break is a web app that allows Joey to conquer fears and anxieties via journaling, answering guiding questions/prompts that are based on what I've been asked during past therapy sessions, and conquering smaller fears/actions that build up to a fear I want to conquer.

OVERVIEW

CONQUER YOUR FEARS

The meat and bones of Limit Break! This is where you can create fear and step structures that will help out with facing fears. Each fear and step have guiding questions that allows to gain insight of the mind.

EXPLORE RESOURCES

Access resources that will help out with the fear-facing process! Learn more about anxiety tips, how to effectively conquer your fears, and common fears/phobias.

FEARS CONQUERED

Keep track of your journey and progress by checking out the fears that have been conquered!
